Who We Are

The ICT Club at LivingSpring Royal Seed College is a community of passionate students dedicated to exploring technology, creativity, and innovation. We provide opportunities for members to learn, collaborate, and showcase their talents in web development, animation, app creation, and more.

Our Mission

To empower students with digital skills that prepare them for the future, foster teamwork, and encourage innovation in ICT-related fields.

What We Do

  • Organize workshops and training sessions on web development, programming, and design.
  • Encourage creativity through animation and multimedia projects.
  • Support students in building apps and digital solutions for real-world problems.
  • Showcase achievements through exhibitions and competitions.
